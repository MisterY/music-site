# Practice Elements

These are the areas that should be practiced/exercised.

* Posture
* Breathing, relaxation
* Technique
* Time
* Sound
* Listening
* Reading
* Writing, transcribing
* Orchestration
* Expression
* Dynamics
* Band playing, playing with others
* Improvisation
    * creating melody
    * composition

## Posture

Practice with a mirror.

## Time

Practice with and without a click.
